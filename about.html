About Mi Lifestyle Marketing

Trusted Direct Selling Ecosystem

Mi Lifestyle Marketing Pvt Ltd is a direct selling company offering health, wellness, personal care, home care, nutrition, and agricultural products through a network marketing model. It operates through brands like Elements, On & On, Neustar, Indiagro, MiHome, and Aahar, focusing on quality products and entrepreneurship opportunities via its distributor network.
